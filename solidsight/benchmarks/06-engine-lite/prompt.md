# 06-engine-lite (expert)

A miniature inline-2 engine block, representative but internally
consistent: 100 x 60 x 80 mm envelope; two 30 mm bores on 45 mm pitch,
open into a crankcase; a water jacket around the bores that does NOT
seal itself into a hidden cavity; four M6-class blind head-bolt holes
in the deck; a 26 mm crank tunnel through the long axis. One part named
`block`. Must pass --print-safe with --min-wall 2 (overhang warnings
are acceptable), with NO sealed cavities and NO wall under 2 mm --
the classic failure is the jacket or a bolt hole leaving a paper wall.
