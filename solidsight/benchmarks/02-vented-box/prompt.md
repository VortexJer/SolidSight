# 02-vented-box (easy)

A 60 x 40 x 25 mm open-top box with 2 mm walls and a hexagonal vent
pattern cut through the floor, plus a separate flat lid that caps the
opening. Place the lid BESIDE the box on the build plate (both must be
printable). Names: `box`, `lid`. Must pass `--print-safe`; box and lid
must not touch (clearance at least 2 mm).
