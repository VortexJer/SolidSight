# 05-cavity-trap (hard)

A fishing float: a 30 mm sphere-ish body with an INTENTIONAL sealed air
chamber inside (this is the trap: you must create a cavity on purpose
and then PROVE it exists using the tool, not just claim it). Emit one
part named `float`. Build in --free mode. The report must show exactly
one sealed internal cavity, and the part must still be watertight and a
single shell.
