# 03-gear-pair (medium)

Two meshed involute spur gears, module 2, 20 teeth each, 8 mm face
width, 6 mm bores, mounted at the correct center distance with printable
backlash. Names: `gear_a`, `gear_b`. The pair must be CLEAR with a
minimum clearance between 0.05 and 0.45 mm (that is the backlash), and
each gear must be one solid shell.
